Design Principles

  • • Clean & Minimal: Let the content and typography breathe with adequate spacing (generous margins & paddings like section-padding).
  • • Subtle Elevation: Prefer soft outlines and subtle shadow shifts over heavy dropshadows. Rely on border-gray-200 for definition.
  • • Trustworthy Palettes: Base the experience heavily on neutral typography (Gray 600, 900) while injecting Primary Blue specifically for progress, branding, and emphasis.
